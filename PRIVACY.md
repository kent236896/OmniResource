# Privacy Policy — OmniResource

**Effective Date:** 2026-03-30
**Author:** TANGKUN

---

## 1. Overview

OmniResource ("the Application") is a desktop media discovery and download tool. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Data You Provide
- **API Keys** — Third-party API keys (Pexels, Pixabay) that you enter in the Preferences page. These are stored **locally on your device only** and are never transmitted to our servers.
- **Search Queries** — Keywords and search parameters you enter are sent directly to the respective third-party platforms (Pexels, Pixabay) via their official APIs.

### 2.2 Data Generated Locally
- **Download History** — Records of your download sessions (session name, keywords, file names, timestamps) are stored in a local SQLite database on your device.
- **Downloaded Files** — Media files downloaded through the Application are saved to a local directory you specify.

### 2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following on remote servers:
- Personal identification information
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Crash reports
- Device identifiers

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The Application communicates with the following third-party platforms when you perform searches:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|---------------|
| Pexels | Stock photo & video search | https://www.pexels.com/privacy-policy/ |
| Pixabay | Stock photo & video search | https://pixabay.com/service/privacy/ |
| Datamuse | Keyword expansion (no account required) | https://www.datamuse.com/api/ |

These services are governed by their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them separately.

---

## 4. Local Data Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device:

- **Database:** A SQLite file (`omniresource.db`) located in the application's data directory.
- **Settings:** Environment variables and configuration stored in a local `.env` file.
- **Downloaded Media:** Stored in the download path you configure in Preferences.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by removing these files.

---

## 5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally, your data security depends on the security of your own device. We recommend:
- Keeping your operating system and the Application up to date.
- Not sharing your API keys with others.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

OmniResource is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
